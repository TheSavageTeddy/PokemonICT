Stats
Pokemon have certain stats that are crucial determinants in how they perform in battle. These stats are split into five categories: HP, Attack, Defence, Special Attack, Special Defence and speed. Attack refers to how much physical damage a Pokemon can inflict, and defence Refers to how affected it will be by a physical attack. Special Attack and special defence Have the same concept, except they refer to special damage, not physical (Pokemon have Moves that are both physical and special). Speed determines which pokemon in a battle go first, and HP (health power) refers to how much damage a pokemon can take before it faints. Finally, the final stat is named BST (meaning base stat total), which is what we use as a factor of sorting pokemon by strength.
